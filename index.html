WCAT × CMC

Exploring Experiential Worlds, Subject Structures, and Structural Freedom

An evolving open research project. Through writing, case observation, dream research, AI experimentation, and open collaboration, we attempt to understand:

Why do different people form different experiential worlds?

Why do some pathways gradually become locked?

Is there such a thing as observable, reconstructible structural freedom?
