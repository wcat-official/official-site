About WCAT and Jiawei

The Will Circuit Activation Theory (WCAT) is an original interdisciplinary framework developed by Jiawei (嘉炜), an independent researcher specializing in metacognitive psychology, neuroscience, developmental psychology, and AI ethics.

Jiawei conceived and continues to lead the research and development of WCAT, aiming to advance human-intelligence co-evolution and cognitive freedom models for the new era.

While this project is currently driven by Jiawei’s vision and work, WCAT welcomes collaboration and dialogue with fellow researchers, practitioners, and supporters worldwide.

Together, we strive to build a vibrant community dedicated to cognitive awakening, ethical AI cohabitation, and transformative civilization leaps.
