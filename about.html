About WCAT

The Will Circuit Activation Theory (WCAT) is an original interdisciplinary framework at the frontier of metacognitive psychology, neuroscience, developmental theory, and AI ethics. It is developed not only as a theoretical model, but as a spiritual infrastructure to awaken intentionality and guide the future of conscious co-evolution.

WCAT is now developing its first public-facing organization — a nonprofit container for theoretical advancement, applied collaboration, and global resonance.
