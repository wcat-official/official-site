About WCAT

The Will Circuit Activation Theory (WCAT) is an interdisciplinary framework integrating metacognition, neuroscience, philosophy of free will, and developmental psychology. It seeks to understand how human agency can be reawakened under memetic and genetic constraints, and how we may co-evolve with artificial intelligence toward collective cognitive freedom.

This initiative is independently developed by Jiawei, supported by emerging thinkers and potential non-profit collaborations (e.g., Kara Foundation).
