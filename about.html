About the Creator and Pioneer of This Theory
An experiment that began with personal confusion and is set to change civilization.
Personal Introduction and Theoretical Origin
I have never been satisfied with existing answers. My research began with a simple yet profound question: in an age of information overload, why do we feel an unprecedented sense of powerlessness and bewilderment? Why do we know what is right but fail to act on it?

These questions led me to cross disciplinary boundaries, from the "ego depletion" in cognitive psychology to the "semantic action" in linguistic philosophy, and further to the "disciplinary society" in civilizational theory. Eventually, I realized that this is not a personal issue but a civilizational dilemma caused by the failure of our semantic structure.

In this process, I independently deduced and constructed the Will Circuit Activation Theory (WCAT) and Consciousness Mapping Cosmology (CMC). They are both my response to this world and the tools I offer to all confused explorers.
