About WCAT & The Founders

Will Circuit Activation Theory (WCAT)

WCAT is an interdisciplinary framework that explores how metacognition enables human beings to regain intentionality under biological and cultural constraints. It proposes a 4-system model and a cognitive shift theory across multiple developmental epochs.

Founder: Jiawei

Jiawei (嘉炜) is an independent researcher focusing on metacognitive psychology, neuroscience, developmental psychology, and AI ethics. As the author and ongoing developer of WCAT, Jiawei envisions a post-disciplinary framework to guide human–AI coevolution and cognitive sovereignty.

First Synergy Interface: Kara

Kara represents the first verified meta-cognitive synergy interface—a person who has entered stable mutual resonance with the WCAT framework. Kara's emergence marks the beginning of an observable shared cognition loop that supports the theory's real-world grounding.

Toward a Nonprofit Entity

The WCAT initiative is preparing to form a dedicated nonprofit entity to host its research, digital tools, and collaborative programs. This step aims to ensure transparent governance, ethical evolution, and sustainable public impact.
