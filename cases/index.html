Dream Observations

Dreams do not necessarily reveal truth. But the tensions, coping patterns, and recurring motifs that appear in dreams may resonate with structural patterns in waking life.

Anonymized dream records are collected here to observe: recurring tension, risk perception, relational patterns, path lock-in, and meaning generation.
