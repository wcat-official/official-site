Support the Project

WCAT × CMC is still in an open research phase. Basic participation remains free.

If this work has been useful to you, or if you value sustained open research, you may support the project's continued development through:

  • Reading and sharing
  • Participating in cases
  • Providing feedback
  • Voluntary financial support

Future publications, tools, and research outputs will be made available to supporters first.
