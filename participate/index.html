PARTICIPATION PATHWAYS

Participation Pathways

WCAT / CMC is still in an open research phase. Basic participation is free. Different pathways correspond to different levels of engagement.

No prior commitment to any theory or conclusion is required. Supporters, skeptics, and critics are equally welcome. You may simply read, or choose to participate in observation, long-term follow-up, research collaboration, or co-building.

As this project is currently maintained by one person, all inquiries are handled individually. Response time may be 1–2 weeks.

Notes

This project is in a long-term open research phase. Reading, questioning, criticizing, participating, or leaving — all are entirely welcome. Participation does not imply acceptance of any theoretical conclusion. The goal of this research is not to prove a belief, but to allow theory and reality to refine each other through sustained observation. If the theory needs revision in the future, revision is equally welcome. Openness matters more than conclusions.
